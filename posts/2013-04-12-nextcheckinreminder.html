Receive a reminder on your next checkin

Posted on 12 April 2013

I often tell me : remind you to pay back Arthur the next time you come at the office. This task does not need to be in my todo list because, it is only usefull when I am at the office.
I needed a reminder to ping me the next time I will be at work.

To fill this gap, I created NextCheckinReminder :

  1. choose a place from Foursquare;

  2. enter the task you need to be reminded.

You’re done : next time you checkin in this place, you will receive this reminder by email.


This application was made in scala with Play Framework and is hosted on Clever Cloud !
